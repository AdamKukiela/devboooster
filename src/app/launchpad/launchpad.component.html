Launchpad

A place for growth.

Launchpads allow experienced Software Engineers to educate Junior Developers and reap the rewards of that commitment. Launchpads are set up as an on-site office that is run by the mentor as a side gig to their day job.

Mentors

Mentors' mission is to first spend months educating the mentees - so that they're not junior anymore - and then subcontracting them on predefined rates. Once hired, mentees get additional support.

Mentees

Mentees are committing to two- year-long Loyalty Agreements as a form of appreciation for free education, and mentors keep growing with their Launchpad - what hinders their need for looking for other opportunities.
